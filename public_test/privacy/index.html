Privacy Policy

Introduction

FreeToolz (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at freetoolz.in.

Information We Collect

No User Accounts

FreeToolz does not require user accounts. All tools run entirely in your browser — no data is sent to our servers.

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, we automatically collect certain information when you visit:

  • Log data — browser type, referring pages, time of visit, IP address (anonymized)
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent on pages, tool interactions

Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Theme preference — stored locally in your browser to remember dark/light mode
  • Google AdSense cookies — for personalized advertising (if you consent)
  • Essential cookies — for basic site functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Improve our tools and content
  • Display relevant advertisements (via Google AdSense)
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns
  • Maintain site security and prevent abuse

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to our website or other websites. You can learn more about how Google uses your data at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Opting Out of Personalized Ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google’s Ads Settings. You can also disable third-party cookies in your browser settings.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of data collection for advertising purposes
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

Children’s Privacy

FreeToolz is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
