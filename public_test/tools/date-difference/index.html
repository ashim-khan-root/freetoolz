How to Use the Date Difference Calculator

  1. Select start date — choose from the date picker
  2. Select end date — choose your target date
  3. View the difference — total days, months, years, and business days

Perfect for project planning, contract duration, countdowns to events, and calculating age or tenure.

Features

  • Total duration — years, months, days between dates
  • Business days — count weekdays (Mon-Fri) excluding weekends
  • Weekend days — total Saturdays and Sundays
  • Weeks + remainder — clean breakdown in weeks and days
  • Instant results — updates as you change dates
