## How to Use the Video Bitrate Calculator

1. **Select resolution** — from SD to 8K
2. **Enter frame rate** — from 1 to 120 FPS
3. **Choose codec** — H.264, H.265, H.265+, VP9, or AV1
4. **Select bit depth** — 8-bit or 10-bit
5. **Read bitrate and file sizes** — per minute, hour, day, or custom duration
