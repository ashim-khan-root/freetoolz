## How to Use the Calorie Calculator

1. **Enter your stats** — age, weight, height, gender
2. **Select activity level** — how active you are
3. **Choose your goal** — lose, maintain, or gain weight
4. **Get your numbers** — daily calories + macro split

Understand exactly how many calories you need daily and how to split them between protein, carbs, and fat. All data stays in your browser.

## Features

- **Mifflin-St Jeor BMR** — science-based calculation
- **Weight goals** — lose 0.5/1 lb per week, maintain, or gain
- **Macronutrient breakdown** — protein, carbs, fat in grams
- **Dual units** — imperial and metric
- **Visual pie chart** — see your macro split
