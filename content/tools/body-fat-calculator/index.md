## How to Use the Body Fat Calculator

1. **Select method** — US Navy (most accurate) or simple BMI-based
2. **Enter measurements** — based on the method chosen
3. **Get body fat %** — see your estimated body fat and category

The US Navy method uses neck, waist, and hip measurements for the most accurate estimate. All data stays in your browser.

## Features

- **US Navy method** — most accurate estimation formula
- **BMI-based** — quick estimate if measurements aren't available
- **Body fat categories** — essential, athlete, fitness, average, obese
- **Dual units** — imperial and metric
- **Visual gauge** — see where you fall on the scale
