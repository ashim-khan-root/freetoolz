## How to Use the Flooring Calculator

1. **Enter room dimensions** — length and width
2. **Set waste** — 5-10% for straight, 15% for diagonal
3. **Get results** — total area and material needed

Perfect for homeowners, contractors, and DIYers installing hardwood, laminate, vinyl, tile, or carpet. All calculations happen in your browser.

## Features

- **Square footage/area** — total flooring area needed
- **Waste allowance** — customizable waste percentage
- **Box calculator** — enter box coverage to get box count
- **Multiple shapes** — rectangle and L-shaped rooms
- **Unit conversion** — feet or meters
