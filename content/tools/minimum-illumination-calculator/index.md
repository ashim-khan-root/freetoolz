## How to Use the Minimum Illumination Calculator

1. **Enter camera min. illumination** — found in the camera datasheet (e.g. 0.05 lux for color, 0.01 lux for B&W)
2. **Select camera mode** — color or B&W/IR (IR mode typically has 3× better sensitivity)
3. **Choose scene type** — from bright sunlight to starlight
4. **Or enter custom lux** — override scene selection with your measured value
5. **Get instant rating** — excellent, good, adequate, marginal, or insufficient

- **Camera-to-scene matching** — compares spec to real-world lighting
