## FAQ

### What units does this converter support?
It supports milligrams (mg), grams (g), kilograms (kg), metric tons (t), pounds (lb), ounces (oz), stones (st), and US tons. Most everyday weight conversions are covered.

### Is the conversion accurate?
Yes. The tool uses standard conversion factors (e.g., 1 kg = 2.20462 lb). Results are calculated with high precision and rounded to a clean decimal format for readability.

### Can I convert ounces to grams?
Yes. Select ounces as the source unit and grams as the target. For reference, 1 ounce equals approximately 28.35 grams.

### Does it work offline?
Once the page loads, all conversion logic runs in your browser. You can use it without an internet connection.

### What is the difference between mass and weight?
Mass measures the amount of matter in an object (kg, g, lb-mass). Weight measures the force of gravity on that mass. On Earth, the terms are used interchangeably in everyday contexts. This converter handles mass units.
