5. **Copy the result** — click the result field to copy the converted value to your clipboard for use in recipes, calculations, or documentation.

## FAQ

### What's the difference between US and UK gallons?
A US gallon equals 3.785 liters, while a UK (imperial) gallon equals 4.546 liters — about 20% larger. The converter includes both options. When converting recipes or measurements, verify which gallon system your source uses.

### How many cups are in a liter?
One liter equals approximately 4.23 US cups or 3.52 UK (imperial) cups. The exact value depends on which cup standard you are using.

### Is the converter accurate for cooking?
Yes. All conversion factors are precise to standard definitions. For cooking, slight variations in cup sizes across different countries can affect results, but the converter uses the internationally accepted US cup (240ml) and UK cup (284ml) standards.

### Can I convert volume to weight?
This tool converts between volume units only. Volume-to-weight conversion requires knowing the density of the specific substance (e.g., 1 liter of water weighs 1 kg, but 1 liter of oil weighs about 0.9 kg).
