## How to Use the Fuel Cost Calculator

1. **Enter trip distance** — in kilometers
2. **Set fuel efficiency** — your vehicle's km/L
3. **Enter fuel price** — current price per liter
4. **Select vehicle type** — petrol, diesel, CNG, or electric
5. **Add commute details** — trips per month and one-way distance

## Features

- **Total fuel cost** — cost for the trip
- **Fuel required** — liters needed
- **Cost per km** — per-kilometer running cost
- **Monthly commute cost** — based on trips and distance
- **Efficiency comparison** — see how you compare to the average for your fuel type
- **Multi fuel type** — petrol, diesel, CNG, electric
