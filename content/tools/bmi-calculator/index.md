## How to Use the BMI Calculator

1. **Enter your weight** — in pounds or kilograms
2. **Enter your height** — in feet/inches or centimeters
3. **Get instant BMI** — see your score and category

Understand your BMI category: Underweight (&lt;18.5), Normal (18.5-24.9), Overweight (25-29.9), or Obese (30+). All calculations happen in your browser — nothing is saved or uploaded.

- **Instant calculation** — updates as you type
- **Dual units** — imperial (lbs/ft) and metric (kg/cm)
- **BMI categories** — color-coded results
- **Healthy weight range** — shows ideal weight for your height
- **Visual indicator** — gauge-style display
