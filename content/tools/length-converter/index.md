## FAQ

### Which units does this converter support?
The converter supports millimeters (mm), centimeters (cm), meters (m), kilometers (km), inches (in), feet (ft), yards (yd), miles (mi), and nautical miles (nmi). More units can be added based on user demand.

### How accurate are the conversions?
All conversions use standard conversion factors with high-precision arithmetic. Results are displayed to 6 decimal places, which is far more precise than any practical application requires.

### Why are there different measurement systems?
The metric system (meters, kilometers) is used in most countries worldwide. The imperial system (feet, miles) is primarily used in the United States. Some countries like the UK use a mix of both systems.

### Is there a difference between nautical miles and regular miles?
Yes. A nautical mile (1,852 meters) is based on the Earth's circumference and is used in aviation and maritime navigation. A statute mile (1,609.34 meters) is what you see on road signs in the US and UK.
