## FAQ

### Why is heading hierarchy important for SEO?
Search engines use heading structure to understand the topical hierarchy of your content. A clear H1 > H2 > H3 structure helps them index your page correctly and can improve rankings for target keywords.

### Is it okay to have multiple H1s?
HTML5 allows multiple H1s per page, but most SEO experts recommend one H1 per page for clarity. The tool flags multiple H1s so you can make an informed decision.

### What is a skipped heading level?
A skipped level happens when you jump from H2 to H4 without an H3 in between. This breaks the semantic structure and can confuse both search engines and assistive technologies.

## Tips

- Use your H1 for the main page title (it should match your title tag)
- Start each major section with an H2 and break subsections into H3s
- Avoid going deeper than H4 — if you need H5 or H6, consider restructuring your content
