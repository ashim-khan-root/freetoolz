## How to Use the Sleep Cycle Calculator

1. **Choose mode** — wake-up time (when should I sleep?) or bedtime (when should I wake up?)
2. **Enter your target time** — when you want to wake up or go to bed
3. **Adjust fall-asleep time** — how long it typically takes you to fall asleep
4. **See recommendations** — bedtimes/wake times for 4, 5, and 6 sleep cycles

## Features

- **Wake-up mode** — enter wake time, get recommended bedtimes
- **Bedtime mode** — enter bedtime, get recommended wake times
- **90-minute cycle tracking** — based on standard sleep cycle research
- **Fall-asleep buffer** — accounts for the time it takes to fall asleep
- **Recommendations highlighted** — 5 and 6 cycles (7.5-9 hours) are ideal
