## How the Cost Estimator Works

The estimator combines current market pricing for surveillance equipment with your specific requirements:

1. **Camera Configuration** — select quantity, resolution (2MP to 12MP), and camera type (bullet, dome, turret, or PTZ)
2. **Recording Setup** — choose NVR channel count, storage retention days, and recording mode
3. **Infrastructure** — add PoE switching, UPS backup, cabling, and installation
4. **Instant Results** — see itemized costs per component, total equipment cost, and optional MOI-compliant configuration

## MOI Compliance Option

For installations requiring compliance with Ministry of Interior (MOI) standards, the estimator includes:
- 120-day storage retention minimum
- RAID 5 or RAID 10 configuration
- UPS backup for all critical equipment
- Approved camera models and specifications
