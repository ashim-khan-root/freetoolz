## FAQ

### How do I enter a mixed number like 2 1/2?
Enter the whole number separately from the fraction. Convert 2 1/2 to an improper fraction: (2 × 2 + 1)/2 = 5/2. Enter 5 as numerator and 2 as denominator. The calculator accepts improper fractions directly.

### Why does the calculator simplify fractions?
Simplification shows the result in its most reduced form — the smallest possible numerator and denominator. This is the standard way to present fraction answers in math and makes comparisons easier.

### What happens if I enter 0 as the denominator?
Division by zero is undefined in mathematics. The calculator will show an error if you try to enter a zero denominator. Always ensure your denominator is a non-zero number.

### Can this calculator handle multiple operations at once?
The calculator processes two fractions with one operation at a time (A + B, A - B, A × B, A ÷ B). For more complex expressions with multiple operations, do one step at a time, using each result as an input for the next operation.

### How do I divide fractions?
To divide by a fraction, multiply by its reciprocal (flip it upside down). For example, 2/3 ÷ 3/4 = 2/3 × 4/3 = 8/9. The calculator shows this step so you can see exactly how it works.
