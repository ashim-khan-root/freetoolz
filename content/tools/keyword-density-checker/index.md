## FAQ

### What is the ideal keyword density?
There's no fixed ideal percentage, but most SEO experts recommend 1-3% for your primary keyword. Focus on writing naturally for readers — if your content sounds forced, you're probably over-optimizing.

### What are n-grams?
N-grams are contiguous sequences of n words. 2-grams (bigrams) show two-word phrases like "content marketing" or "SEO tools." 3-grams (trigrams) show three-word phrases. Analyzing n-grams helps you understand the topical phrases in your content.

### Does keyword density still matter for SEO?
Keyword density is less important than it was a decade ago, but it's still a useful diagnostic. Modern SEO focuses on topical relevance and semantic search. Use the density checker as one signal among many, not as a strict target.

### Why are stop words filtered?
Stop words (the, and, is, in, etc.) are so common they'd dominate the frequency table and hide meaningful keywords. Filtering them gives you a clear view of your actual content keywords.

## Tips
