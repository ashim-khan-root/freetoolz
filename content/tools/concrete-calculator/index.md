## How to Use the Concrete Calculator

1. **Select shape** — slab (rectangle), column (cylinder), or footing
2. **Enter dimensions** — length, width, depth
3. **Instantly see** cubic feet, cubic yards, and bags needed

Perfect for contractors, DIYers, and construction pros. All calculations happen in your browser — nothing is uploaded.

## Features

- **Multiple shapes** — slab, column, and footing modes
- **Unit conversion** — feet/inches or meters/centimeters
- **Bag calculator** — shows bags for 40lb, 60lb, 80lb, and 90lb mixes
- **Total weight** — estimated weight of concrete needed
- **Instant results** — updates as you type
