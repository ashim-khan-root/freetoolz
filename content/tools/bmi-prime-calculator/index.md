## How to Use the BMI Prime Calculator

1. **Enter your weight** — in kilograms
2. **Enter your height** — in centimeters
3. **Select gender** — used for reference ranges
4. **View results** — BMI, BMI Prime, category, and healthy weight range

## Features

- **BMI calculation** — standard body mass index
- **BMI Prime** — ratio to normal upper limit (25). 1.0 = normal boundary
- **Visual meter** — see where you fall on the BMI scale
- **Category label** — underweight, normal, overweight, or obese
- **Healthy weight range** — recommended weight for your height
- **Color-coded feedback** — green (normal), yellow (borderline), red (high)
