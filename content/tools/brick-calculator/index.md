## How to Use the Brick Calculator

1. **Enter wall dimensions** — length and height
2. **Choose brick type** — modular, queen, king, or custom
3. **Set mortar joint** — standard 3/8" or custom
4. **Get count** — number of bricks plus mortar estimate

Perfect for masons, contractors, and DIY brickwork projects. All calculations done in your browser.

## Features

- **Multiple brick types** — modular, queen, king, utility, custom
- **Block option** — CMU block sizes available
- **Mortar estimate** — cubic feet of mortar needed
- **Waste allowance** — includes 10% for breakage
- **Paver mode** — for patios and walkways
