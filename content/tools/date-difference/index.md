## FAQ

### Does the calculator include both the start and end dates?
By default, it calculates the full period between two dates excluding the end date. For same-day calculations this shows 0 days. To include both dates, simply add 1 to the total.

### How are business days defined?
Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding Saturday and Sunday. Holidays are not excluded — the calculator doesn't account for public holidays, so subtract those manually if needed for your region.

### Can I calculate age with this tool?
Yes — enter a birth date as the start date and today's date (or any future date) as the end date. The calculator will show the exact years, months, and days, which is the standard age format.

### What's the maximum date range supported?
The calculator works with dates between January 1, 1900 and December 31, 2099. For historical dates outside this range, the results may not be accurate due to calendar system changes.

### Does it account for leap years?
Yes — the calculator properly accounts for February 29th in leap years (years divisible by 4, except centuries not divisible by 400). The date picker also allows selecting February 29 on leap years only.
