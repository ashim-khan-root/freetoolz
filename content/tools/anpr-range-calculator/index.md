## How to Use the ANPR Range Calculator

1. **Select camera resolution** — from 2MP to 12MP
2. **Choose sensor size** — standard CCTV sensor formats
3. **Enter lens focal length** — mm of the lens you're planning to use
4. **Select target plate size** — EU, US, Asia, or UK standards
5. **Choose required PPM level** — detection, recognition (ANPR), or identification
6. **Enter test distance** — see what PPM and pixels you'll get at that range

## Features

- **Max ANPR distance** — theoretical maximum range for reliable plate reading
- **PPM at any distance** — pixels per meter at your chosen test distance
- **Pixels across plate** — whether you have enough detail for LPR
- **Three performance tiers** — detection (75 ppm), recognition (125 ppm), identification (250 ppm)
- **Real-time adjustments** — see how changing lens or sensor affects range
