## FAQ

### How accurate is the age calculator?
It's accurate down to the second. The tool accounts for leap years, varying month lengths, and time zone differences based on your local system time.

### Can I calculate age for someone who hasn't been born yet?
Yes — pick a future date of birth and any reference date. The calculator will show a negative age or countdown until birth.

### Does this tool handle leap years?
Yes. February 29 is handled correctly, and the calculator accounts for all leap years between the birth date and reference date.

### What's my age in months if I'm 25?
25 years = 300 months (25 × 12). The calculator shows this breakdown automatically along with weeks and days.
