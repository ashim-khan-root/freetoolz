## How to Use the Readability Score Tool

1. **Paste your text** — article, blog post, or any content
2. **Instant analysis** — word count, sentences, and syllable count
3. **Read Flesch Reading Ease score** — 0 (very difficult) to 100 (very easy)
4. **Check grade level** — what education level is required to understand it

## Features

- **Flesch Reading Ease** — standard readability metric (0-100)
- **Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level** — US school grade equivalent
- **Reading level description** — from Very Easy to Very Difficult
- **Word and sentence stats** — average words per sentence
- **Syllable counting** — automated syllable detection
