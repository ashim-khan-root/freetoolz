## How to Use the Macro Calculator

1. **Enter your stats** — weight, height, age, and gender
2. **Select activity level** — from sedentary to very active
3. **Choose your goal** — lose weight, maintain, or gain weight
4. **View your macros** — daily calories, protein, carbs, and fat

## Features

- **BMR calculation** — Mifflin-St Jeor equation
- **TDEE estimation** — total daily energy expenditure based on activity
- **Goal-based adjustment** — -500 kcal (lose) or +300 kcal (gain)
- **Macro breakdown** — 30% protein, 40% carbs, 30% fat
- **Gram values** — protein and carbs in grams (4 kcal/g), fat in grams (9 kcal/g)
