## How to Use the Tip Calculator

1. **Enter bill amount** — total bill before tip
2. **Choose tip %** — percentage or custom amount
3. **Split between** — how many people
4. **Get results** — tip per person, total per person, and grand total

Perfect for restaurants, group outings, and service calculations.

## Features

- **Per-person split** — divide tip and total evenly
- **Quick tip presets** — 10%, 15%, 18%, 20%, 25%
- **Real-time** — updates as you type
- **No data sent** — everything runs locally
