## How to Use the Tip Calculator

1. **Enter bill amount** — type the total bill amount before tip. This is the base amount shown on your receipt before any gratuity is added.
2. **Choose tip percentage** — select a preset percentage (10%, 15%, 18%, 20%, or 25%) or enter a custom percentage. The tip amount and total update instantly.
3. **Set number of people** — enter how many ways to split the bill. The calculator divides both the tip and the total equally among everyone.
4. **Review the breakdown** — see four key values: tip amount, total bill (with tip), amount per person, and tip per person.
5. **Adjust as needed** — change any field and all values recalculate in real time. No need to start over if someone adds an extra item to the order.

## FAQ

### What tip percentage should I use?
Standard tipping varies by country and service quality. 15-20% is common in US restaurants. 10% is typical for buffets or below-average service, while 20-25% is appropriate for excellent service or large groups. In many other countries, service charge is included.

### Is the tip calculated on the pre-tax or post-tax amount?
Most tipping guidelines suggest calculating the tip on the pre-tax bill amount. Enter the subtotal from your receipt (before tax) for the most accurate tip calculation.

### How does splitting work?
The calculator divides the total bill (including tip) equally by the number of people. Each person pays the same amount. If people ordered items of different values, you may want to handle the base bill separately and split only the tip.

### Does the calculator work for any currency?
Yes. The tool works with any currency — simply enter the numeric amount. The values displayed are pure numbers, so you can interpret them in USD, EUR, GBP, INR, or your local currency.

## Tips

- For a quick 20% tip, double the tax amount (in many US states where sales tax is around 8-10%) — but use the calculator for precise amounts
- When dining with couples, split by household rather than by individual to keep the math fair and simple
- Save the custom percentage feature for exceptional service — setting 25% is generous and appreciated by service staff
