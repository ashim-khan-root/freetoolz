## How to Use the Loan Calculator

1. **Enter loan amount** — principal amount you want to borrow
2. **Set annual interest rate** — percentage per annum
3. **Choose loan term** — in years
4. **Select loan type** — standard reducing balance or interest-only
5. **View results** — EMI, total interest, total payment, and amortization table

## Features

- **Monthly EMI** — standard reducing balance formula
- **Total interest & total payment** — see the full cost of borrowing
- **Interest percentage** — what portion of total payment is interest
- **Amortization schedule** — first 12 months showing principal vs interest breakdown
- **Interest-only option** — for interest-only loan calculations
