## How to Use the Cron Generator

1. **Pick a preset** — common schedules like "every 15 minutes" or "daily at 9 AM"
2. **Edit individual fields** — minute, hour, day of month, month, day of week
3. **Or type directly** — enter a cron expression in the text field
4. **See next 5 runs** — preview when your schedule will fire
5. **Copy the expression** — use it in your crontab, CI/CD, or scheduler

## Features

- **10 presets** — common cron schedules ready to use
- **Individual field editors** — build expressions visually
- **Direct expression input** — paste and edit any cron string
- **Next run preview** — see the next 5 scheduled executions
- **Natural language description** — human-readable schedule explanation
