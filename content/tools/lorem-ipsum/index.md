## FAQ

### What is Lorem Ipsum?
Lorem Ipsum is placeholder text derived from a Latin passage written by Cicero in 45 BC. It has been the standard dummy text in the printing industry since the 1500s and is used to demonstrate visual form without relying on meaningful content.

### Why not just use "copy here" text?
Lorem Ipsum has a natural-looking distribution of letters and word lengths that resembles readable English. This makes layouts look more realistic than repetitive placeholder text, so you can better judge spacing, flow, and visual balance.

### Is Lorem Ipsum real Latin?
It's scrambled Latin from Cicero's "De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" (The Extremes of Good and Evil). The standard passage has been altered over centuries, so it's not grammatically correct Latin — but that's part of the point, since it shouldn't distract from the design.

### How much text should I generate?
For a typical website section, 1-3 paragraphs is enough. For typography testing, generate 30-50 words. For full-page mockups, generate 5-10 paragraphs. Start small and add more if needed.
