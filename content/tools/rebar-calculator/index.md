## How to Use the Rebar Calculator

1. **Enter slab dimensions** — length and width
2. **Set spacing** — horizontal and vertical grid spacing
3. **Choose rebar size** — from #3 (10mm) to #8 (25mm)
4. **Get results** — number of bars, total length, and weight

Perfect for contractors, engineers, and construction estimators. All calculations happen in your browser.

## Features

- **Bidirectional grid** — calculates both directions
- **Multiple rebar sizes** — #3 through #8 (metric too)
- **Weight estimate** — total rebar weight in lbs or kg
- **Unit conversion** — feet/inches or meters
- **Overlap allowance** — accounts for lap splices
