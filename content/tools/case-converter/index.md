3. **Copy** the result instantly with the one-click copy button

## FAQ

### When should I use Title Case vs Sentence case?
Title Case capitalizes every major word — use it for article titles, blog headlines, and book names. Sentence case capitalizes only the first word — use it for body text, email content, and casual writing.

### Does Title Case handle small words correctly?
Yes. The tool intelligently handles articles (a, an, the), conjunctions (and, but, or), and prepositions (in, on, at) — keeping them lowercase unless they're the first or last word in the title.

### Can I use this for bulk text conversion?
The tool handles any amount of pasted text. For very large documents, paste in sections to verify the conversion is correct.

### What does tOGGLE cASE do?
Toggle case inverts the case of every letter — uppercase becomes lowercase and vice versa. It's useful for quickly fixing text typed with the Shift key held at the wrong moments.
