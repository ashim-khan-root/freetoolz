## How to Use the URL Encoder/Decoder

1. **Type or paste** text in the input area
2. **Click Encode** to URL-encode or **Decode** to URL-decode
3. **Copy** the result with one click

All processing happens in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.

## Features

- **URL Encode** — convert special characters to %XX format
- **URL Decode** — convert %XX codes back to readable text
- **One-click copy** — copy result to clipboard
- **100% private** — everything runs in your browser
