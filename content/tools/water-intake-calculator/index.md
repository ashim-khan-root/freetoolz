## How to Use the Water Intake Calculator

1. **Enter your weight** — in pounds or kilograms
2. **Select activity level** — how much you exercise
3. **Choose climate** — adjust for hot weather
4. **Get your target** — daily water intake in oz or liters

Proper hydration is essential for health, energy, and performance. This calculator gives you a personalized daily water goal based on your body and lifestyle.

## Features

- **Weight-based formula** — scientific hydration guidelines
- **Activity adjustment** — replaces fluids lost through sweat
- **Climate adjustment** — more water in hot/humid conditions
- **Multiple units** — ounces, cups, liters, milliliters
- **Daily reminders** — suggested drinking schedule
