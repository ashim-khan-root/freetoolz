## How to Use the Compound Interest Calculator

1. **Enter principal** — starting amount
2. **Set interest rate** — annual percentage rate
3. **Choose time period** — number of years
4. **Toggle compounding** — simple or compound interest
5. **Get results** — final balance, total interest, and yearly breakdown

Perfect for investment planning, savings goals, and understanding the power of compounding.

- **Simple & Compound** — toggle between both modes
