## FAQ

### What is the difference between the three modes?
"What is X% of Y?" finds a portion of a number (e.g., 15% of 200 = 30). "X is what % of Y?" finds the percentage relationship (e.g., 30 is 15% of 200). "% change" finds the increase or decrease as a percentage (e.g., 50 to 75 is a 50% increase).

### Can I calculate percentage increases?
Yes. The "% change" mode handles both increases and decreases. Enter the original value and the new value — the calculator shows the percentage difference and indicates whether it is a rise or fall.

### Is this calculator accurate for decimal numbers?
Yes. The calculator handles decimal inputs and displays results with appropriate precision. For financial calculations, results are typically shown to two decimal places.

### Can I use this offline?
