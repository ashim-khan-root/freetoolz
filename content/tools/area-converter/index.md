## FAQ

### How many square feet are in an acre?
There are 43,560 square feet in one acre. This is a standard US survey measurement.

### What's the difference between a hectare and an acre?
A hectare is 10,000 square meters (about 2.47 acres). Hectares are used in the metric system, while acres are used in US customary and imperial systems.

### Can I convert between any area units?
Yes. The tool supports all common area units including square meters, square kilometers, square feet, square yards, square miles, acres, and hectares.

### Is this tool accurate for legal documents?
The calculator provides mathematically precise conversions. However, for legal real estate transactions, always verify measurements with a licensed surveyor.
