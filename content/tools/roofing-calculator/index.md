## How to Use the Roofing Calculator

1. **Enter roof dimensions** — length and width of each roof plane
2. **Set roof pitch** — rise in inches per 12 inches of run
3. **Get materials** — squares, shingle bundles, underlayment, cap

Perfect for roofing contractors, homeowners planning a replacement, and material estimators. All calculations run in your browser — nothing is uploaded.

## Features

- **Roof pitch adjustment** — accounts for slope factor
- **Squares calculation** — industry standard (1 square = 100 sq ft)
- **Bundle count** — estimates based on 3 bundles per square
- **Underlayment** — #15 felt roll count
- **Ridge cap** — linear feet of cap shingles
- **Waste allowance** — automatically adds 10%
