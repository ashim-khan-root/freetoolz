## How to Use the Timezone Converter

1. **Select source timezone** — choose your starting timezone from the dropdown. You can search by city name, country, or UTC offset. Common options include your local time, Asia/Dubai, US/Eastern, or Europe/London.
2. **Enter the time** — set the hours and minutes you want to convert. The time uses a 24-hour format for clarity, or you can toggle to 12-hour AM/PM mode.
3. **Select target timezone** — pick the destination timezone you want to convert to. The converted time appears instantly as you make your selection.
4. **View converted time** — the tool shows the equivalent time in your target timezone, along with the UTC offset and any daylight saving adjustment for both timezones.
5. **Swap timezones** — click the swap button (⇄) to reverse source and target for quick round-trip checks.

## FAQ

### Does this tool account for Daylight Saving Time?
Yes. The converter automatically detects whether DST is in effect for each timezone and adjusts the offset accordingly. Results reflect the actual current time difference.

### How many timezones are supported?
The converter includes all major timezones covering every populated region on Earth, including half-hour and quarter-hour offset timezones like UTC+5:30 (India) and UTC+12:45 (Chatham Islands).

### What happens if the converted time falls on a different day?
The tool clearly displays the date alongside the converted time, so you can see if 9:00 AM Monday in New York becomes 5:00 PM Monday or 2:00 AM Tuesday in Dubai.
