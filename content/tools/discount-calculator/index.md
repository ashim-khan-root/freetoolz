## How to Use the Discount Calculator

1. **Enter original price** — full price of the item
2. **Enter discount %** — percentage off
3. **Get results** — sale price, savings amount, and discounted percentage

Perfect for shopping, budgeting, and finding the best deals.

## Features

- **Sale price & savings** — see exactly what you pay and save
- **Discount percentage** — reverse-calculate discount from original and sale price
- **Real-time** — updates as you type
- **No data sent** — everything runs locally
