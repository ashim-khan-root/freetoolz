## How to Use the Electricity Bill Calculator

1. **Add your appliances** — select from 18 common home/office appliances
2. **Enter quantity and hours/day** — for each appliance you use
3. **Or enter kWh directly** — if you already know your consumption
4. **Set your tariff** — slab rates (default) or custom per-unit rate
5. **View your bill** — total consumption, energy charge, fixed charge, and total

## Features

- **18 appliance presets** — bulbs, fans, ACs, fridge, TV, laptop, and more
- **Slab-based pricing** — 5-tier slab structure (customizable by rate input)
- **Custom flat rate** — override slab pricing with a single per-unit rate
- **Manual kWh input** — enter consumption directly
- **Detailed breakdown** — see which slab tier you fall into
- **Fixed charge** — includes monthly fixed connection fee
