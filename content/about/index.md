## What Is FreeToolz?

FreeToolz is a collection of **free online tools** designed to help you get things done quickly. From health calculators to developer utilities, every tool runs entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded to any server.

## Why We Built This

We noticed that most "free" tools online are cluttered with ads, require signups, or limit what you can do unless you pay. FreeToolz is different:

- **No signup required** — just open and use
- **No server uploads** — everything runs in your browser
- **No artificial limits** — use tools as much as you want
- **100% free** — no hidden charges, no premium tiers

## What You'll Find

- **Health Calculators** — BMI, BMR, calorie, body fat, water intake
- **Developer Tools** — JSON formatter, SQL formatter, hash generator, minifiers
- **Construction Calculators** — concrete, brick, paint, roofing, rebar
- **CCTV & Security** — bandwidth, storage, PoE, UPS calculators
- **Finance Tools** — EMI, compound interest, ROI, discount calculator
- **Text & Conversion Tools** — case converter, unit converter, URL encoder
- **SEO Tools** — meta tag generator, keyword density checker, sitemap generator

## Our Values

**Privacy first.** We don't collect your data. Every tool runs client-side.

**Simplicity.** Clean interfaces, no clutter, no distractions.

**Free forever.** No plans to monetize beyond non-intrusive ads.
