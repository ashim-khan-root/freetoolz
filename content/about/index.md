## What Is FreeToolz?

FreeToolz is a comprehensive collection of **100+ free online tools** designed to help you get things done quickly and efficiently. From health calculators to developer utilities, security estimators to SEO analyzers — every tool runs entirely in your browser. Nothing you enter is uploaded to any server, ever.

## Why We Built This

Most "free" tools online are cluttered with ads, require signups, impose usage limits, or send your data to third-party servers. FreeToolz was built to be different:

- **No signup required** — just open and use. No accounts, no emails, no passwords.
- **No server uploads** — every tool runs client-side using JavaScript. Your data stays on your device.
- **No artificial limits** — use tools as much as you want, as often as you want.
- **100% free** — no hidden charges, no premium tiers, no paywalls. Always free, forever.
- **Privacy first** — we don't collect, store, or share the data you enter into any tool.

## Our Quality Standards

Every tool on FreeToolz undergoes the following before publication:

1. **Functional testing** — does the calculation produce correct results?
2. **Cross-browser testing** — works on Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari
3. **Mobile responsiveness** — tools are usable on phones and tablets
4. **Accessibility basics** — keyboard navigable, readable contrast ratios
5. **Performance review** — loads in under 2 seconds on standard connections
